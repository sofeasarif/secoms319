About Us

Culture & Identity Based Organization.

The Ames Student Association for Malaysians (ASAM) represents as the official Malaysian student organization here at Iowa State University since 19XX. The goal and mission of ASAM is to promote Malaysian culture and traditions to the diverse community here at Iowa State University to students, faculty and staff, while also being the backbone for all Malaysian students being a second home away from home.


What We Do!


Supporting New Journeys


We are dedicated to helping fellow Malaysians feel at home in their new journey abroad. Whether you're a student, professional, or expatriate, we offer a welcoming community and a range of services to assist you in navigating the challenges and opportunities of life in a foreign country.

View details »


Cultural Connections and Events


We host cultural events and activities that bring a piece of Malaysia to your new surroundings. From festive celebrations of Malaysian holidays to cultural exchange programs, we aim to maintain a connection to your roots while embracing new experiences.

View details »


Practical Assistance and Resources


Our organization provides practical support, including information on local resources,housing assistance, and career development opportunities. We're here to help you with the practical aspects of your journey and ensure that you thrive in your new environment.

At Ames Student Association for Malaysians (ASAM), we believe that our events play a pivotal role in creating a vibrant and supportive community for our fellow Malaysians abroad.
